Book Free Trial or Inclusive Consultation

Enrollment Steps

  • Create parent account with COPPA consent at /register.
  • Book either a 45-minute child trial or a 30-minute parent consultation.
  • Receive session link and attend with your chosen educator.
  • Activate subscription and track progress from parent dashboard.
