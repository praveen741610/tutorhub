Teaching Methodology

Structured, Personal, and Trackable

Every class follows a clear objective, personalized delivery, and measurable review cycle.

Small Batch Size

Limited learners per class to ensure individual attention and interaction quality.

One-on-One Sessions

Personalized support for difficult topics, exam prep, or ASD-specific interventions.

Recorded Classes

Students can revisit sessions for revision and parents can monitor lesson continuity.

Homework Tracking

Consistent assignments with review loops and actionable correction feedback.

Monthly Assessment

Regular tests to measure concept retention and identify focused support areas.

Progress Reports

Parent-friendly reports covering academics, behavior goals, and communication growth.

Delivery Workflow

  • Baseline assessment and goal setting.
  • Weekly lesson plans with class objectives.
  • Task submission, review feedback, and reinforcement.
  • Monthly evaluation and parent review call.
