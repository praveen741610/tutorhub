About Us

Where CBSE Rigor Meets Inclusive Care

We support Indian-origin students abroad with structured academics and specialized autism-friendly learning support.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become the most trusted global CBSE learning academy for NRI families while setting benchmarks in inclusive education.

Mission

Deliver measurable academic outcomes and personalized developmental growth through high-quality teaching and parent partnership.

Founder Message

"Every child deserves a curriculum that is both structured and compassionate. We built this academy to ensure NRI children stay connected to Indian academic excellence, while students with ASD receive the dignity, patience, and specialist support they need to thrive."

Why CBSE for NRI Kids?

  • Clear, progressive syllabus that builds strong fundamentals.
  • Smooth continuity for families relocating back to India.
  • Board-exam readiness with disciplined concept coverage.

Why Autism-Focused Support?

  • Students with ASD benefit from predictable routines and individualized pacing.
  • Integrated academics + behavioral + communication support improves long-term outcomes.
  • Parent counseling ensures learning continuity beyond classroom hours.
